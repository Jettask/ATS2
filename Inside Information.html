Inside information 

The ATS HQ is located in Berlin supporting 400 employees with the rest of the workforce being remote.

 The remote workers work from home in their own respective countries/territories and at customer sites. 

This creates its own issues with document sharing printing and scanning. As well as securely connecting their laptops to public/home networks.

 IT have a challenge keeping inventory on all PC’s, Laptops. New employees and associates receive a laptop from a pool of used equipment when they start working with the ATS. 

ATS doesn’t really want to invest into laptops due to the heavy rotation of staff. This is due to seasonal/project activities that may only last for a temporary period. 

Equipment can often end up damaged, so they are looking for a solution to this problem. 

 The main reason remote workers come to the office is for training. 

ATS needs a solution for issuing, receiving, and processing invoices. 

As ATS have limited resources and local capacity in material and personnel and have issues with managing and monitoring endpoint devices centrally as they have many different devices and models. 

To cover the needs of remote workers and the home office workers during the pandemic they integrated MS Office 365. They started using Teams for meetings with great success. Due to this success, they have merged the majority of their support business to Microsoft Azure.
